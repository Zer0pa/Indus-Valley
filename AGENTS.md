# AGENTS.md

This repo is the staged standalone surface for `gnosis-indus`.

## Mission

Preserve and extend the Indus lane as an anchor application and evidence repo.
Do not turn it into a decipherment story, a generic platform story, or a fake
standalone search product.

## Read order

1. `AGENTS.md`
2. `PRD_GNOSIS_INDUS_ANCHOR_APPLICATION.md`

## Non-negotiable rules

1. The PRD is sovereign.
2. Runtime truth and artifact truth outrank prose.
3. Keep the non-decipherment posture explicit.
4. Keep the Phase 4 stability caveat explicit, including when citing later
   k=100 paper work.
5. Treat search-without-decode as an in-repo application surface.
6. Do not silently widen rights claims for images, corpora, or references.
7. Do not absorb other workstreams just because shared code exists.
8. Do not revert unrelated work from other agents.

## Boundary notes

- `authority/` contains copied verdict and review documents. Update it only with
  explicit source replacement, never by paraphrase.
- `src/gnosis_indus/search_surface/` is the current first runtime slice;
  `src/gnosis_indus/catalogue/` and `src/gnosis_indus/falsification/` remain
  later extraction targets.
- `docs/family/INDUS_EXPORT_CONTRACT.md` defines what downstream bundles may
  claim.
- `_internal/MIGRATION_PLAN.md`, `SOURCE_BOUNDARY.md`, `DATA_POLICY.md`, and
  `tools/indus_ops_gate.py` are binding handover surfaces, not optional
  commentary.

## What success looks like

The repo becomes more standalone without becoming less truthful.
